About Berkshire Hathaway Inc.

Welcome to Berkshire Hathaway Inc., a renowned multinational conglomerate led by Warren Buffett and Charlie Munger. With over five decades of excellence, we manage a diverse portfolio of businesses, aiming for long-term growth and shareholder value through integrity, innovation, and sustainability.
Our legacy is built on prudent investments, strategic acquisitions, and a commitment to superior returns. Guided by visionary leadership, we navigate economic cycles and market challenges, ensuring stability and strength.
Our diverse businesses span industries such as insurance, utilities, manufacturing, transportation, and retail. We uphold values of integrity, excellence, long-term perspective, responsibility, and teamwork, fostering a culture of collaboration and inclusivity.
Join us on our journey of growth and shared success, as we continue to build a brighter future together.
